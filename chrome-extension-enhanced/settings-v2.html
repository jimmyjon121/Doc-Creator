Why AI Enhancement?

While our rule-based extraction is highly capable, AI enhancement can:

• Understand context and nuance in complex descriptions

• Extract implicit information not explicitly stated

• Identify unique program differentiators

• Validate and corroborate extracted data

Note: The extension works excellently without AI. AI is optional enhancement only.
