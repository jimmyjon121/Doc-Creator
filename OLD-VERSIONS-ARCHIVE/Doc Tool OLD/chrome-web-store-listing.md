### PRIVACY POLICY

**Family First Program Extractor - Privacy Policy**

**Data Collection:** This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

**Local Processing:** All information extraction happens locally in your browser. When you click "Extract Page Info," the extension reads the current webpage content and processes it entirely within your browser.

**Temporary Storage:** Extracted information is stored temporarily in your browser's local storage only for the purpose of copying to clipboard. This data is not transmitted anywhere and is automatically cleared when you close your browser.

**No Analytics:** We do not use any analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

**No External Communication:** The extension does not communicate with external servers except when you choose to open the Doc Creator tool.

**User Control:** The extension only processes webpage content when you explicitly click the "Extract Page Info" button. It does not run automatically or in the background.

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: support@familyfirstas.com
